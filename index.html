How a session works

One room. 48 hours.
Either you act, or you let go.

  1. 01

    The room opens

    Three times a week. Around 10 men, 10 women. Everyone walks in at the same time.

  2. 02

    Two icebreakers

    Up to two opening messages per session. Two. Make them count. Each one opens a chat.

  3. 03

    Choose someone

    Pick one of your chats. If they choose you back, every other chat closes. You leave the session together. You're out of the room. No new session until you unchoose.

  4. 04

    Or let it go

    If nobody chooses, the chats expire with the room. The next one opens in 48 hours.
